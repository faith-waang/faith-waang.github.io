BRANCHE




Webapp Interface Design




I was a part of a pitch team that was given the problem space of antiquated workplace organization technologies.

Our solution was Branche, a web-app that provided a platform for mid-size companies to facilitate interactions between management and employees in a creative and comfortable manner. The key feature of the web-app is the ability for management to find idle employees who are interested in side projects, and for idle employees to find different roles in the company to engage in.

As the UI designer for this project, I coordinated with our branding and graphic designer across the span of a week to create this mockup in Figma. For our final pitch, the functions of our web-app were animated to simulate real-life interaction with this application.
